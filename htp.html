How to play Flag-It!
  • Flag-It! is a fun and educational game for all, it has over 230 flags from around the world, the aim of the game is you match the flag to the country/principality
  • The game picks a random flag from the 233 available flags and then shows you 4 possible answers
  • You just have to click on the right answer
  • Dont worry if you get the answer wrong, Flag-it! will show you the correct answer
  • There are 10 questions in each game by defult
  • But you can change this in the options if you want more or less questions
  • The flags are selected randomnly from all 230(ish) countries.
  • The game keeps scores of right and wrongs answers
