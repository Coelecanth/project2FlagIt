How to play Flag-It!

  • Flag-It! is a fun and educational game for all, it has over 240 flags from around the world, the aim of the game is you match the flag to the country/principality
  • Each question shows a flag and then you have 4 possible answers to select from
  • You just have to click on the right answer, and then click "Next"
  • Dont worry if you get the answer wrong Flag-it! will show you the correct answer
  • There are 25 questions in each game
  • The flags are selected randomnly from all 240(ish) countries.
  • The game keeps scores of right and wrongs answers
